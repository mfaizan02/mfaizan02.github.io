About

Muhammad Faizan (Urdu: محمد فیضان‎; born 9 May; mostly referred to as 'Faizan') is a Pakistani singer, songwriter and electronic music composer. He is studying BS Sofware Engineering from PUCIT, Lahore. Muhammad Faizan was born in Faisalabad, Pakistan in 2002. He passed his matriculation from City High School (Boys Campus) in 2018, intermediate from Punjab Group of Colleges (Batala Colony Campus) in 2020.
